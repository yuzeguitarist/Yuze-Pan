Yuze Pan

Born in Hangzhou in July 2011, began studying classical guitar with Professor Le Li in May 2018.

Won first prize in the Children's Group at the Changsha International Guitar Festival Competition in July 2019.

Admitted to the fourth grade of the Primary School Affiliated to the Central Conservatory of Music in September 2020 with the highest professional score, studying under Associate Professor Jiajiong Li.

Enrolled in the Middle School Affiliated to the Central Conservatory of Music in September 2023 with the highest professional score.

Won first prize in the Under-14 Youth Category at the 12th "Juan Crisostomo de Arriaga" International Guitar Competition in Spain in May 2024.

Won first prize in the Under-14 Youth Category at the 13th "Andres Segovia" International Youth Guitar Competition in Germany in June 2024.

Received the First-Class Award for Outstanding Professional Achievement at the Middle School Affiliated to the Central Conservatory of Music in November 2024.
